Using the Mini Map

The Mini Map is located to the left of the main window and displays a view of the entire map. Units and resources on the map are color-coded:

Dark Blue

Light Blue

You can click and drag in the Mini Map to rapidly scroll your view in the main window. For other ways to scroll the view see Navigating the Map.

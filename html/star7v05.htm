Trigger Tutorial: Step Five

Starcraft provides you with a list of conditions to choose from in the pull-down menu located at the top of this dialog.

When you select a condition, the full text of the condition appears in the box just below the pull-down menu. The text of the condition describes, in plain English, what that condition will be looking for in the game.

STEP: Open the pull-down menu by clicking on its arrow.

Step 5: Enhancing the Map

You now have two platforms for players and a bridge between them. Now is the time to enhance the map.

Explore the various other terrain types. Lay down some more terrain. Create and connect more platforms. Experiment with different elevations and looks. If you even make a mistake, just go to the Edit menu and select Undo.

Place additional resources on the map. Players often need to expand past their first colony and need resources to do so.

When you are satisfied with the general layout of your map, spice up terrain with Doodads such as ramps, solar collectors and other interesting objects.

When you are done, it is time to save and play your map in Starcraft.
